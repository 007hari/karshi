Welcome to Rohit Krishi

ROHITKRISHI INDUSTRIES PVT. LTD. under brand ROHITKRISHI has diverse range of products which include manual, animal, and tractor drawn multi-crop seed cum fertilizer planters, sugarcane sett cutter planter, seedling transplanter, potato planter, ginger & turmeric planter, no-till seeder, roto-planter, garlic planter, vibro-subsoiler etc. We emerged as one of the leading manufacturer of farm equipments in India with brand name ROHITKRISHI.

Having more than 100 dealers, Rohitkrishi reaches out to farmers in Maharashtra, Karnataka, Andhra Pradesh, Telangana, Madhya Pradesh, Gujrat etc. states in the country. Understanding the need for good after sales service, we ensure effective after sales-support to our growing customer base through our business partners. Our Manufacturing Plant is situated at Pune in the Akudri Industrial Estate of PCMC MIDC Area. The plant is well-equipped with the state of the art set up for manufacturing of farm equipments. The set up is supported by the advanced technology and best in class quality control systems.

This facility works in close proximity with our in-house Research and Development Division. Together, the Manufacturing and R&D Teams aim at producing innovative and path-breaking solutions in the seeding and planting segment. The technical set up is backed by skilled and highly experienced professionals in multiple domains and subject expertise areas. We are proud of our accomplishments and look forward to working with our employees, our business partners and our customers to build a brighter future together.
